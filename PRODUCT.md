# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Women in Johannesburg (and online) at transitional life moments: pregnancy, postpartum, spiritual awakening, or a desire to reconnect with the body and self. They are not browsing casually — they arrive with intention, often at tender or significant points in their lives. The primary action is booking a session or making an enquiry.

## Product Purpose

EmbraceIN is a wellness practice led by Samantha-Lee Barker and a small team, offering yoga, meditation, birth doula support (IVF, prenatal, birth, postpartum), children's classes, feminine empowerment workshops, and community events — all based in Beverley, Sandton, Johannesburg. The site exists to make Sam and her team feel real, trustworthy, and worth reaching out to, then convert that trust into a booking or conversation.

## Brand Personality

Mystical, feminine, transformative. Not soft or apologetic — rooted and specific. The energy of a woman who has done the work and holds space for others to do theirs.

## Anti-references

- Generic wellness-industry aesthetic: sage green palettes, flowing Playfair script headlines, stock photos of white women in warrior pose on a beach
- Spa/clinic sterility: cold whites, sans-serif minimalism that feels removed from the body
- Busy spiritual-chaos: every element competing — crystals, gradients, overlapping textures, too many typefaces

## Design Principles

1. **The practitioner is the product.** Sam's presence, story, and energy should be palpable on every page — not hidden behind brand abstraction.
2. **Trust earned through specificity.** Especially for birth services, women in vulnerable moments need to feel seen. Use concrete language, real stories, and design that doesn't flinch from depth.
3. **Transformation is physical.** EmbraceIN works through the body. Design should feel embodied — weighted, textured, sensory — not airy or abstract.
4. **Sacred, not generic.** The brand holds deep personal and cultural meaning. Every choice should be deliberate and specific to this practice, not borrowed from wellness-industry defaults.
5. **Community before transaction.** The goal is belonging. Booking is the outcome of connection, not the opener.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum throughout. Birth and doula services reach women at physically and emotionally significant moments — clarity, legibility, and ease of navigation are non-negotiable. Respect reduced-motion preferences.
